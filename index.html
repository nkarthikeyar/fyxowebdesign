Why Do We Still Paint Our Walls White?

Let us explore the emotional and practical reasons behind India's relationship with white walls.

In Luxury Homes

White feels aspirational, like a gallery.

Clean, sophisticated, and timeless - but often cold and impersonal.

In Middle-Class Homes

White feels safe and practical.

Easy to maintain, affordable to repaint, but lacks personal expression.

In Rental Homes

White feels temporary and expected.

The default choice that never truly feels like home.
