Camera Logic

Step 2

1. Capture

The user points the camera at a wall with floor and vertical edges visible.

2. Detect

Vision logic identifies anchor lines, perspective tilt, and wall boundaries.

3. Scale

Distance cues are converted to a real-world scale estimate.

4. Output

The app returns a readable height value for immediate planning use.
